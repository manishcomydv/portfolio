Services

From design to development, my services are built to help you grow faster and smarter.

Custom Shopify Store Development

Get a fully customized Shopify store tailored to your brand and business needs. From unique themes to powerful features, I build stores that are fast, user-friendly, and designed to convert visitors into customers.

Theme Customization & Design

Stand out with a Shopify store that reflects your brand perfectly. I customize existing themes or design unique layouts to create a visually stunning, user-friendly experience that keeps your customers engaged.

Store Migration to Shopify

Seamlessly move your existing store to Shopify without losing data, design, or functionality. I handle product transfers, customer data, orders, and SEO to ensure a smooth, secure, and efficient migration process.

Shopify App Integration

Enhance your store’s functionality with powerful app integrations. From marketing tools to inventory management, I help you choose, install, and configure the right apps to streamline operations and boost performance.

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

Boost your sales without increasing traffic. I analyze user behavior, optimize store layout, and improve key touchpoints to turn more visitors into loyal customers — helping you get the most out of every click.

Third-Party API Integration

Connect your Shopify store with third-party services like CRMs, ERPs, payment gateways, and more. I integrate APIs to automate workflows, sync data, and enhance your store’s capabilities with reliable and secure connections.

Ongoing Maintenance & Support

Keep your Shopify store running smoothly with regular updates, bug fixes, and technical support. I offer reliable ongoing maintenance to ensure your store stays secure, optimized, and up-to-date at all times.
